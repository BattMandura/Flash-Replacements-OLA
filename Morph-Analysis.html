Morphological Analysis

Introduction

This is a learning task, not a test. It helps you explore compounding. Don't worry if you don't get it right the first time. The idea is to learn.

For each word, try to perform the following 2 activities:

1. Identify the morphological boundaries for each word. Full instructions on how to do this are available by clicking on the "instructions" button at the bottom of the next screen.

2. Build a morphological tree by correctly combining lexical items to form a word. Full instructions on how to do this are avaiable by clicking on the "instructions" button at the bottom of the next screen.

To check your solution, click the "check" button. After three unsuccessful attempts, the correct answer will be provided for you. If you have answered the question correctly, move on to the next word by clicking on the next arrow at the bottom of the screen.
